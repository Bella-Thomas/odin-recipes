Grilled Peaches and Cream

Description

Peaches and Cream is an easy and sweet dessert that originated in England. After they've been grilled id you add a little drizzle of extra honey it makes it even more perfect and adds a little extra sweetness to the taste.

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted

  • 2 tablespoons of clover honey

  • 1 cup of soft cream cheese with honey and nuts

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time

8 mins

Total time

23 mins

Serves 8

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill on medium high heat.
  2. Brush peaches with a light coating of oil.
  3. Place pit side down onto the grill. Grill them for 5 minutes, or until the surfaces have nice grill marks.
  4. Turn the peaches over, and drizzle with a bit of honey. Place a dallop of cream cheese spread in place of the pit.
  5. Grill ofr 2-3 more minutes, or until the filling is warm.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
