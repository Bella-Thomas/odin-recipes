Air-fried Italian Stuffed Tomatoes

Description

Garlic, Parmesan cheese, fresh basil, and toasted nuts turn brown rice into a superstar stuffing in this Italian-inspired stuffed tomato side dish that's cooked in the air fryer.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice

  • 1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese

  • 1/4 cup chopped toasted walnuts

  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1/4 cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs, divided

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

If preferred you can substitute shredded mozzarella cheese for goat cheese.

Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time

15 mins

Total time

30 mins

Serves 4

Method

  1. Cut tops off tomatoes and scoop flesh with a melon baller, leaving 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick sides and bottoms. Discard tomato tops and flesh.
  2. Brush the bottom of an air fryer basket with olive oil.
  3. Combine cooked rice, Parmesan cheese, goat cheese, walnuts, 1 tablespoon basil, and garlic in a medium bowl. Combine bread crumbs, and 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a small bowl. Fill tomatoes with rice mixture, then sprinkle with bread crumb mixture. Place stuffed tomatoes into the prepared air fryer basket.
  4. Cook in the air fryer at 370 degrees F (188 degrees C) until tomatoes are tender, filling is heated through, and topping is golden brown, about 15 mins. Garnish with the remaining basil.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
